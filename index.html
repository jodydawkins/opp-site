One small protocol

Presence without captivity.

OPP describes where a person or organization exists online. It does not own identity, host content, replace existing services, or create another social network.

Independently hosted

A presence document can live on infrastructure controlled by the person or entity it represents.

Cryptographically signed

An identity signs its presence document so clients can verify the declaration instead of trusting a platform.

Implementation-neutral

Any application or language can implement the published specification without permission or centralized approval.
